1. Keep child calm and still. Sit them down.
2. Apply gentle but firm pressure with clean cloth.
3. If cloth soaks, add more layers on top.
4. Keep child’s head upright.
5. If cut is deep, bleeding continues, or child seems drowsy → get medical help.

**Don’ts:** Do not shake child, do not press hard on obvious skull injuries.

**When to call emergency:** Heavy bleeding, fainting, confusion, vomiting, or signs of skull injury.
